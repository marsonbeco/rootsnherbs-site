About rootsnherbs.online

A free educational resource on diabetes, blood sugar, and metabolic health.

🎯

Our Purpose

rootsnherbs.online exists to make evidence-based information about diabetes and blood glucose management accessible to everyone — in plain, jargon-free language.

We believe that people with diabetes, prediabetes, or metabolic health concerns deserve access to clear, accurate, science-based education. Our content is designed to inform, not to sell. We do not promote, recommend, or sell any products, supplements, devices, or services.

📋

Our Content Standards

Every article on this site is written according to the following principles:

  • Research-backed: Claims are supported by peer-reviewed studies, preferably randomized controlled trials and meta-analyses. We cite study sources and indicate the quality of evidence.
  • Honest about uncertainty: Where evidence is mixed or limited, we say so. We do not overstate findings to sound more authoritative.
  • No miracle claims: We do not use language like "cure," "reverse," or "guarantee." These terms misrepresent the current state of science.
  • Reviewed regularly: Medical knowledge evolves. We aim to update articles when significant new evidence emerges.
  • Free of commercial influence: We have no sponsored content, affiliate relationships, or paid promotions. Our editorial decisions are not influenced by commercial interests.
🔬

Primary Sources We Rely On

Our content draws primarily from the following trusted sources:

  • American Diabetes Association (ADA): diabetes.org — Standards of Medical Care in Diabetes (updated annually)
  • World Health Organization (WHO): who.int — Global diabetes guidelines and epidemiology
  • PubMed / National Library of Medicine: pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov — Primary research literature
  • Cochrane Library: cochranelibrary.com — Systematic reviews and meta-analyses
  • Diabetes Care (journal): A leading peer-reviewed journal publishing original diabetes research
  • The Lancet Diabetes & Endocrinology: High-impact clinical research in diabetes
⚕️

Full Medical Disclaimer

The information on rootsnherbs.online is provided for general educational and informational purposes only. It is not intended to be, and should not be used as, a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Always seek the advice of your physician, endocrinologist, or other qualified healthcare professional with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition or health objective. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay in seeking it because of something you have read on this website.

If you believe you may have a medical emergency, call your doctor or emergency services immediately.

rootsnherbs.online does not recommend or endorse any specific tests, products, procedures, opinions, or other information that may be referenced on this website. Reliance on any information provided by rootsnherbs.online is solely at your own risk.

Individual responses to dietary, lifestyle, and other interventions vary significantly. What works for one person may not work for another, and any intervention that affects blood glucose levels should be undertaken under appropriate medical supervision, particularly for people taking medications that lower blood glucose.
